FAQ

What types of vehicles and aircraft do you sell?
We offer a wide selection of luxury vehicles, including but not limited to, sedans, SUVs, and sports cars, private jets, helicopters, and commercial aircraft.
Do we cater to international customers?
We orient our services such that the location of our customers does not play a role in the quality of service they recieve. We offer 24/7 online and call-in assistance, and have a wide network of approved service partners to ensure you never have to worry about transportation again.
What warranties come with the purchase?
We pride ourselves in having one of the most gracious warranty programs in the aviation sales industry. While the warranty varies from product to product, we cover mechanics fees at no extra cost for the first few years after the purchase of a new jet, and provide full coverage (parts and labour) when you purchase one of our ground transportation vehicles as well for a period of 5 years.
